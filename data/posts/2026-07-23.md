# POST 2 — Personal Post

**Topic:** The difference between owners who get top dollar and those who don't

---

Two owners sell the same size franchise. One gets 40% more than the other.

Same brand. Same revenue. Different outcome.

I've closed over 100 of these deals, and the gap almost never comes down to luck.

It comes down to preparation.

The owners who get top dollar start getting ready two or three years before they sell. Clean books. Documented systems. A business that runs without them in the building every day.

The owners who leave money on the table wait until they're burned out. They decide to sell on a Tuesday and want to be out by summer.

Buyers can smell that.

Last year I worked with a seller who couldn't hand me clean financials. He mixed personal expenses into the business for years. We spent six months just untangling it, and the delay cost him real money at the table.

Here's the takeaway. Your exit price is being decided right now, by the habits you keep every day.

You don't need to sell tomorrow to start building value today.
