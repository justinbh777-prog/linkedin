## Hook Types (A/B Test Each Post)

**Type A — Financial Hook**
Lead with a transaction size, multiple, or unit count.
> "A 47-unit franchise just sold for 8 figures."

**Type B — Contrarian/Pattern Hook**
Lead with a market observation that challenges assumptions.
> "The smartest franchise buyers aren't buying restaurants anymore."

**Type C — Urgency Hook**
Lead with a shift happening right now.
> "Private equity is quietly flooding into home services. Here's what they're not telling you."
