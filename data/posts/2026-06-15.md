# POST 2 — Personal Post

**Topic:** What buyers actually look for that most sellers never prepare

---

Most sellers spend years building the wrong thing.

They focus on revenue. Buyers care about something else entirely.

Here is what I see after 100+ deals: buyers are not buying your sales. They are buying how easily the business runs without you.

That is the part most owners never prepare.

I had an owner with strong numbers. Great location. Loyal customers. But every decision ran through him. Scheduling, vendors, hiring, the books. All him.

Buyers looked and saw a job, not a business. The offers came in low.

Compare that to a smaller operator I worked with. Less revenue. But he had a manager running daily operations, clean systems, and documented processes.

He got a higher multiple. On a smaller business.

The lesson is simple. A buyer wants to step in without the lights going off when you leave.

So ask yourself one question. If you took a 60 day vacation tomorrow, would your business survive?

If the answer makes you nervous, that is your roadmap. Fix that before you ever list.
