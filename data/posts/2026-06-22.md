# POST 2 — Personal Post

**Topic:** What I wish every franchise owner knew about SBA financing

---

Most franchise deals don't die over price. They die over SBA financing.

Here's what I wish every owner knew.

The bank isn't just looking at your buyer. They're looking at your business. Your books, your add-backs, your cash flow. If those numbers are messy, the loan stalls before it starts.

I've watched clean deals fall apart because the seller couldn't prove their real earnings. The buyer was ready. The price was agreed. But the bank wouldn't move.

On the flip side, I've seen owners get full asking price simply because their financials were tight and easy to verify.

One owner I worked with kept perfect records for three years before selling. The loan got approved fast. The buyer trusted the numbers. The deal closed early.

That's the lesson. SBA lenders fund clarity, not promises.

If you're thinking about selling, start cleaning up your books now. Not next quarter. Now.

The work you do today decides whether your buyer can actually get funded later.
