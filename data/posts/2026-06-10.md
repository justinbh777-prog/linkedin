# POST 2 — Personal Post

**Topic:** Why the best time to think about your exit is right now

---

The best time to sell your franchise is before you want to.

I know that sounds backwards. Let me explain.

In 20 years and 100+ deals, I've noticed something. The owners who get the best price are the ones who started thinking about their exit years early.

The ones who struggle? They wait until they're burned out, or sick, or just done. They sell from a position of need, not strength.

Buyers can smell that.

I worked with an owner last year who called me the week he decided to quit. Great business. But his books were messy and his numbers had slipped because he'd already checked out months before.

He left real money on the table.

Compare that to owners who plan ahead. They clean up their financials. They build a team that runs without them. They time the market instead of reacting to it.

Here's the takeaway. Planning your exit early doesn't mean you're selling tomorrow. It means you'll be ready when the right moment comes.

And you'll get paid more for being ready.
